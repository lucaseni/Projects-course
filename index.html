Ayrton Senna

Time line of Ayrton Senna:

  • He was born 21 March 1960
  • Won the 1983 British Formula Three Championship
  • He made his Formula One debut with Toleman-Hart in 1984
  • In 1988, he joined Frenchman Alain Prost at McLaren-Honda so Senna won the World Championship
  • 1990 and 1991 he was World Champion again
  • 1 May 1994 he died
